Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Sweet, perfectly chewy, and hard to resist, this famous American chocolate chip cookie is a treat no matter what age or occasion. Enjoy it with a glass of cold milk.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 0.75 cup white sugar
  • 0.75 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat oven to 375F (190C)
  2. Combine flour, baking soda and salt in a small bow.
  3. Beat butter, white and brown sugar and vanilla in a large bowl until creamy.
  4. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake in oven until golden brown, about 9 to 11 minutes, switching rack halfway through.
  8. Cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
  9. Serve and Enjoy
