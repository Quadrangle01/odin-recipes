Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Sweet, perfectly chewy, and hard to resist, this famous American chocolate chip cookie is a treat no matter what age or occasion. Enjoy it with a glass of cold milk.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 0.75 cup white sugar
  • 0.75 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
